Summary of Energy Consumption Analysis

This summary provides an overview of the cleaned dataset and key
findings derived from the energy usage of three buildings (A, B, and C)
over a 72‑hour period.

1.  Data Overview

-   Total records after cleaning: 216 rows
-   Buildings monitored: Building A, Building B, Building C
-   Time range: Hourly readings across 3 days

2.  Key Insights

-   Building B has the highest average consumption across the period.
-   Building C shows the lowest minimum consumption values, indicating
    more variability.
-   Total kWh usage ranks: Building B > Building A > Building C.
-   Peak hours show a clear upward trend in all three buildings,
    suggesting daytime operational loads.

3.  Summary Statistics (Overall)

-   Metrics included: Mean, Min, Max, Total kWh per building
-   These insights help identify high‑usage buildings and potential
    optimization points.
